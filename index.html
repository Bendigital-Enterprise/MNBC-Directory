Greetings Family and welcome to our Church directory. In this directory you will find contact details of all members of Meanwood Ndeke Baptist Church. As an Eldership, we are delighted to present this directory to you and we pray that, as a member of our Church, this directory will be very useful in your hands for ministry to the saints at MNBC.

The Writer to the Hebrews exhorts us as follows in Hebrews 10:24. Let us think of ways to motivate one another to acts of love and good works. The encouragement that the Writer of Hebrews gives here is for us to explore ways we can use to motivate (or spur) one other towards two things: acts of love and good works. In the case of the apostles and the New Testament saints, visiting was a priority way to motivate one other towards acts of love and good works. In the event that the opportunity to visit was not availed, they wrote a letter (communicated).

This directory provides us at least two ways we can use to fulfill this encouragement: visiting and communicating (call, text or email). Therefore, Let us use it to motivate one another to acts of love and good works by visiting and communicating with one another.
